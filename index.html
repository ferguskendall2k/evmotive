Services

01

Control Systems

Embedded control system development from requirements capture through to production calibration. ECU software, model‑based design, HIL verification, and vehicle integration.

  • Control strategy & model‑based design
  • Embedded software (C / AUTOSAR)
  • HIL & SIL test environments
  • Calibration & vehicle commissioning
02

Wiring Design

Full electrical architecture and harness design for prototype, low‑volume, and production programmes. From topology through to manufacturing release data.

  • Electrical architecture & topology
  • Schematic & harness drawings
  • Connector & component selection
  • Manufacturing release (BOM, form‑boards)
03

Engineering Consulting

Technical advisory and specialist engineering resource for vehicle control programmes — from feasibility studies through to on‑programme delivery.

  • Feasibility & concept studies
  • Programme technical leadership
  • Supplier technical review
  • Specialist engineering secondment

About

EV Motive was founded in 2022 to bring senior automotive control‑system and electrical‑design expertise to electric and advanced vehicle programmes. We work with vehicle manufacturers, tier‑one suppliers, and specialist motorsport and performance teams across the United Kingdom and Europe.

Engagements range from short technical consultancy briefs through to full‑programme design delivery. We bring modern toolchains, disciplined process, and production‑grade attention to detail to every brief.
